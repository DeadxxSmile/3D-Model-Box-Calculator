  ------------------------------------------------
|             3D Model Box Calculator              | 
  ------------------------------------------------   

 This script will calculate the values for a box to   
 be created using a 3D printer. You will be prompted  
 to enter the dimensions of the box, lid, and windows.

------------------------------------------------   

 First, pick the type of box you want to create.      
    (1) Box without Lid or Windows
    (2) Box with Lid
    (3) Box with Lid and Windows

 Enter Box Type Option(1,2 or 3): 3

------------------------------------------------    

 Select the filament type being used:
    (1) PLA
    (2) ABS
    (3) PETG
    (4) Nylon
    (5) TPU
    (6) Polycarbonate
    (7) HIPS
    (0) Enter Custom Shrinkage Factor

 Enter the number corresponding to your filament type: 0
 Enter the custom shrinkage factor: 0.994825861994

-------------------------------------------------- 

 Enter the height of the item: 31.6
 Enter the width of the item: 24.6
 Enter the depth of the item: 20.7
 Enter the gap for the item: 0.15
 Enter the number of items: 4
 Enter the thickness of the outer walls: 2.5
 Enter the thickness of the inner walls: 1.8

--------------------------------------------------

 Enter the depth of the lid: 6.25
 Enter the thickness of the lid walls: 1.5
 Enter the separation between the lid and the box outer wall: 2.75
 Enter the gap between the lid and the box: 0.15

--------------------------------------------------

 Enter the overhang of the window: 1.8
 Enter the overhang of the window separators: 0.3
